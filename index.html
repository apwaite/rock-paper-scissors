Rock Paper Scissors Game

How To Play:

  • Rock beats scissors. Paper beats rock. Scissors beats paper.
  • Select the rock paper or scissors button to begin.
  • 5 games total.
  • Best of 5 rounds so there can be a tie!
  • Results will be displayed each round and at the end.
  • To reset the game, press F5 on your keyboard or refresh your webpage.
